Call for Papers

The call accepts papers in French and in English Proposals, of 250 words, should be sent by email to the organizing committee at: moroccanfulbrightalumni@gmail.com

Key dates

    September 13th, 2016: Authors of selected proposals will be informed. November 16th, 2016: Participants send complete texts of papers (max. 8000 words). We solicit your original contribution for presentation at the MFAA General Conference from all subfields related to the conference theme: “Immigration: from Integration to Citizenship”. We also encourage students to submit work in progress.

    Submission Guidelines

    The abstract should not exceed 250 words and must include:
    • Full name(s) of author(s)
    • Institutional affiliation(s)
    • Electronic address(es)
    • Proposed title
    • A structured abstract with three separate sections:
      • Background: the context and purpose of the study;
      • Description: the main findings;
      • Conclusions: brief summary and potential implications.
    Please do not use abbreviations or references in the abstract. The oral presentation will be for 15 minute talks.

    Poster Session

    Each poster will be assigned a number. There will also be lists of the abstract for the poster with the numbers in the book of abstract for the symposium. Please put your poster on the poster board corresponding to the number assigned. The maximum poster size is 91 x 152 cm.
